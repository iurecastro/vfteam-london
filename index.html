About VF Team London

Discover our academy, our philosophy, and what makes us different.

Welcome to Our Academy

At VF Team London, we are dedicated to teaching authentic Brazilian Jiu Jitsu in the Gracie Barra tradition. Our academy is a place where individuals of all ages and skill levels can learn self-defense, improve fitness, and become part of a supportive community.

Founded by Professors Vinicius Franklin and Bruno Deodato, both originally from Belo Horizonte, Brazil, we bring decades of combined experience in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. Our teaching philosophy emphasizes technique, discipline, and respect.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced practitioner, you'll find a welcoming environment where you can grow both on and off the mats.

Our Professors

Learn from authentic Brazilian Jiu Jitsu masters with decades of experience.

Vinicius Franklin

Head Professor - Black Belt

Originally from Belo Horizonte, Brazil, Professor Vinicius has been training in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu for over 20 years. He is a certified Gracie Barra instructor with extensive competition experience.

His teaching style focuses on technical precision and practical self-defense applications, making complex techniques accessible to all students.

Bruno Deodato

Professor - Black Belt

Also from Belo Horizonte, Brazil, Professor Bruno brings a wealth of knowledge in both gi and no-gi Jiu Jitsu. With 18 years of training experience, he specializes in competition preparation and advanced techniques.

He is passionate about helping students develop their unique Jiu Jitsu style while maintaining the core principles of leverage and technique.
